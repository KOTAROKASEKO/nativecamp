My Book Reviews

Recent books I've read and recommend

The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue

V. E. Schwab

A woman cursed to be forgotten by everyone she meets for 300 years. What is the "something" she finds across time?

I was struck by the loneliness of being forgotten and the strength of will to exist anyway.

The bargains with the devil and the way she finally found to leave her "mark" were particularly impressive. It was an epic story about time-transcending love, art, and memory.

Project Hail Mary

Andy Weir

A scientist wakes up on a spaceship with amnesia. With only a clue that he must save Earth, he cooperates with an unknown lifeform.

It's truly a "sci-fi movie you can read." The meticulous scientific detail and the thrilling plot kept me turning the pages.

I was especially moved by the friendship between the protagonist and "him," a bond that transcended species. It's the best sci-fi entertainment I've read in years, full of humor and a love for science. Amazing, amazing, amazing!
